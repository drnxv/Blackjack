RULES

BLACKJACK

  • Keep drawing cards keeping track of the sum
  • The objective is to get as close to 21 as possible without crossing it
  • You have the option to quit at any point
  • The dealer will then play and the closest score to 21 wins
  • Kings, Jacks, and Queens are worth 10 points
  • Aces are either 1 or 11, dealer will announce before round
